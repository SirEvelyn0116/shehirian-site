Spiced Lentil Soup

Description: Comforting, protein-rich, and perfect for fall.

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Total Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté diced onion and sliced carrots with cumin until softened.
  3. Add red lentils and enough water to cover.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes until lentils are soft.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot.
