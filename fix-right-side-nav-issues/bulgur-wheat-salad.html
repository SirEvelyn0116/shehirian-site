Bulgur Wheat Salad

Description: Fresh herbs, lemon dressing, and hearty bulgur.

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bulgur wheat
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Soak bulgur in boiling water for 10 minutes until tender.
  2. Drain excess water and let cool.
  3. Mix bulgur with chopped parsley and mint.
  4. Add diced cucumber.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  6. Toss well and serve chilled.
