Our Certifications

GFSI-Recognized Food Safety Standards

Committed to Excellence

At Shehirian Bulgor Inc, we maintain the highest standards of quality and food safety. Our certifications are recognized by the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI), demonstrating our commitment to providing you with products that meet the most rigorous international food safety standards.

BRC Global Standard

The British Retail Consortium (BRC) Global Standard for Food Safety is one of the world's leading food safety certification programs. Our BRC certification ensures we meet strict requirements for product safety, quality, and operational criteria.

SQF - Safe Quality Food

The Safe Quality Food (SQF) Program is a rigorous, internationally recognized food safety and quality program. Our SQF certification demonstrates our commitment to meeting the highest standards of food safety management.

FSSC 22000

The Food Safety System Certification (FSSC 22000) is a complete food safety management system based on ISO 22000. This certification ensures our entire supply chain meets international food safety requirements.

IFS Food Standard

The International Featured Standards (IFS) Food is a GFSI-recognized standard for auditing food manufacturers. Our IFS certification confirms our dedication to producing safe, high-quality bulgur wheat products.
