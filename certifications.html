Our Certifications

Quality and compliance you can trust

Committed to Excellence

At Shehirian, we maintain the highest standards of quality and food safety. Our certifications demonstrate our commitment to providing you with products that meet rigorous international standards.

Organic Certified

Our bulgur wheat is certified organic, grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. We follow sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and ensure the purity of our products.

Non-GMO Verified

All our products are verified to be free from genetically modified organisms. We source our wheat from trusted suppliers who share our commitment to natural, traditional farming methods.

Halal Certified

Our products are certified Halal, meeting the dietary requirements of Islamic law. Our entire production process is monitored to ensure compliance with Halal standards.

Kosher Certified

We maintain Kosher certification under rabbinical supervision. Our facilities and processes adhere to Jewish dietary laws, ensuring our products meet strict Kosher requirements.
